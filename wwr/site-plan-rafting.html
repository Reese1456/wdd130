Purpose

The purpose of this website is to promote our business brand, to reach a wider audience and bring in more revenue for the business.

Audience

This will help us to reach a wider audience. We are aware of how easy it is to access information from any where, so we plan to target to optimize our website for mobile devices, as well as desktops and laptops. We cater for most ages, but our main target audience will be anyone above the age of 12 years old, mainly targeting those who are looking for an exciting adventure with either friends or family members and we will be able to cater for families with children older than 5 years old.
